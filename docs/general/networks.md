
**XinFin MainNet**

### Specifications

- Chain id: `50`
- RPC endpoint: `https://rpc.xinfin.network`
- Websocket endpoint: `wss://ws.xinfin.network`
- Consensus: XDPoS
- Block finality: `>75%`
- Consensus nodes: up to 108 (masternodes)
- Genesis block date: `2019-05-31 `
- Transaction fee: gas price 0.25 Gwei
